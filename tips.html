Effective Study Tips

  • Time Management: Create a consistent study schedule and stick to it. Use a planner to organize your tasks.
  • Active Recall: Instead of just rereading notes, try to recall information from memory. This strengthens neural connections.
  • Take Breaks: Use techniques like the Pomodoro method (25 minutes of focused study followed by a 5-minute break) to maintain concentration.
  • Teach Others: Explain concepts to a friend or family member. If you can teach it, you know it well.
  • Limit Distractions: Put your phone away, turn off notifications, and find a quiet place to focus.
